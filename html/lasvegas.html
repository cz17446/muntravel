Trip Summary

Las Vegas, of course, has become synonymous with world-class entertainment, ultracool nightlife, renowned restaurants and luxe shopping venues. Stunning hotels have raised the bar for service and entertainment. Amazing venues showcase world-class entertainers, whether they're on the latest leg of a world tour or they're must-see Las Vegas staples. Cirque du Soleil has breathtaking shows in outstanding hotel theaters up and down the Strip. Blue Man Group; Penn & Teller; David Copperfield; and some of the best magicians, singers, impressionists and tribute acts in the world are here too.

Of course, there are thrill rides, sporting events, helicopter rides to the Grand Canyon, pool parties and golf courses to enjoy as well. Las Vegas is simply a party that never ends. There's so much to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. But take a look at these air and hotel packages and we'll make it easy.
